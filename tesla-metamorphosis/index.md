Tesla Metamorphosis is a modality in which the trained Tesla Metamorphosis practitioner accesses the energy, information and light of the [Tesla Waves](/tesla-wave/) to facilitate the client’s journey of healing and evolving.

There are three modes, or ways of working with the intelligent energies of Tesla Metamorphosis®:

* [Tesla Healing Metamorphosis®](/tesla-healing-metamorphosis)
* [Tesla Soul Communication®](/tesla-soul-communitcation) 
* [Tesla Light Body Metamorphosis®](/tesla-light-body-metamorphosis)

Each mode has a different intent and procedure. All use Tesla waves to access light, energy and information. Each mode is used independently but it is recommended that at least 3 sessions of Tesla Healing Metamorphosis be experienced prior to the experience of Tesla Light Body Metamorphosis.

People experience [Tesla Healing Metamorphosis®](/tesla-healing-metamorphosis) to heal some aspect(s) of themselves, be it physical, emotional, mental and/or spiritual.

In [Tesla Soul Communication®](/tesla-soul-communitcation) there is an opening of communication between the practitioner and the client through meaning, emotions and images. This  communication is beyond words and the client might obtain insight about what is causing his/her health, or other problems. These insights can be life changing.

[Tesla Light Body Metamorphosis®](/tesla-light-body-metamorphosis) initiates consciousness evolution. Evolution can be on all levels: physical, mental, emotional and spiritual, and can be more far-reaching than healing. It is done once in a life time.

The founder of Tesla Metamorphosis® is [Anya Petrovic](http://www.teslametamorphosis.com/?lang=EN&pg_id=16&tt_id=24). More in-depth information about Tesla Metamorphosis on the [Tesla Metamorphosis](http://www.teslametamorphosis.com/) website.
