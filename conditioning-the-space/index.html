    Conditioning the Space

    The benefit a client receives during a Tesla Metamorphosis session is achieved by the practitioner facilitating the flow of the Tesla Waves directly into the space surrounding the client. The practitioner is “conditioning the space” and in so doing enables an energetic connection between the client and The Universal Field of Energy. This is how healing occurs.

    Conditioned spaces have been reported for centuries. They are often associated with places referred to as “sacred,” whether they are powerful spots in nature such as Uluru, holy pilgrimage sites or sites with long histories of healing, deep prayer and meditation. Conditioned spaces can also exist in every day settings where people bring conscious connections.

    We all have experienced a particular place or room having a “special” energy and may have wondered how that energy had come about. What we are experiencing at these times is a conditioned space where the energy is in the spectrum of Subtle Energies.

    Everything in the universe is consists of energy. Physicists recognise that physical atoms are actually made up of vortices of energy that are constantly spinning and vibrating. At its smallest observable level, matter is energy, and this energy exists all around us. Subtle Energies have properties beyond the common energy waves (such as radio waves) that orthodox scientific instruments can measure. All living things have the ability to detect and use Subtle Energies.

Subtle Energies can facilitate positive transformations in behaviour, health and awareness. Transformations occur, in both the short-term and long-term. Highly conditioned spaces of ultra-high frequency energy are associated with higher states of consciousness.

Our heart and connective tissue matrix are the “antennae” which send and receive subtle energies from beyond our physical bodies.
